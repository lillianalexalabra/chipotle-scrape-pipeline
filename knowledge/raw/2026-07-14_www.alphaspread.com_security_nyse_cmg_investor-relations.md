**Beat and momentum:** Chipotle said first-quarter results came in ahead of expectations, with revenue up 7.4% to $3.1 billion and comparable sales up 0.5%, helped by better restaurant execution and new menu items.


**Transaction recovery:** The company said it returned to positive transaction growth in the quarter and saw a further step-up in April, driven by Cilantro-Lime Sauce, the rewards relaunch and ongoing menu innovation.


**Margin pressure:** Restaurant-level margin fell to 23.7%, down 250 basis points year over year, as food inflation, labor inflation and higher operating costs outweighed pricing.


**Full-year view:** Management kept full-year comparable sales guidance at about flat and remained cautious on the consumer backdrop, while signaling upside if current trends hold.


**Strategy rollout:** Chipotle highlighted progress on its Recipe for Growth plan, including AI tools, rewards changes, more frequent LTOs, a stronger hospitality push and new leadership hires in brand and digital.
